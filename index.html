I joined Google Research (Mountain View) in 2013. From 2009-2013, I was with Microsoft Research (Asia) where I led the database team. From 2000-2009, I was with IBM Research (Watson). I received the B.S. and M.S. degrees in Computer Science from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 1994 and 1996, the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from University of California, Los Angeles in June, 2000.
